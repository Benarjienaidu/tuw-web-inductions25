Team Unwired

Team Unwired is a passionate and innovative student Formula racing team dedicated to pushing the boundaries of engineering and performance. With a strong focus on sustainable technologies and electric vehicle design, the team combines creativity, technical expertise, and teamwork to compete at the highest levels. Through rigorous testing, problem-solving, and collaboration, Team Unwired continually achieves remarkable milestones and awards, showcasing their commitment to excellence both on and off the track. By fostering a culture of innovation and learning, the team not only excels in competitions but also inspires future engineers and sponsors to be part of the race towards a cleaner, faster, and more exciting future in motorsport.

Our Misssion

Team Unwired is dedicated to advancing student engineering excellence by designing, building, and competing with innovative Formula-style race cars. Our mission is to provide a hands-on, interdisciplinary learning platform that fosters creativity, technical skills, and teamwork in electric vehicle technology. We strive to push the boundaries of sustainable motorsport by integrating cutting-edge engineering solutions, promoting continuous innovation, and engaging with industry partners and sponsors to accelerate the future of mobility and racing.

Vision

We envision a world where motorsport serves as a testing ground for tomorrow’s technologies — cleaner, faster, and smarter. By embracing sustainability and digital transformation, we seek to redefine what an F1-inspired racing team can achieve, proving that performance and responsibility can go hand in hand.

Theoritical foundation

Wireless Innovation – Traditional race cars depend on wired telemetry; Team Unwired pioneers wireless data transfer, reducing weight and enabling real-time decision-making.
Sustainable Engineering – Hybrid-electric and alternative fuel integration position us at the forefront of eco-conscious motorsport.
Collaborative Learning – By uniting students, professionals, and global partners, Team Unwired creates a knowledge-sharing ecosystem.
Applied Research – Our car is not just a prototype, but a research platform to test aerodynamics, AI, and energy-efficient systems.

Car Model

Car Model Name: Unwired X1 (example, can be customized)
Chassis Design: Lightweight carbon-fiber monocoque for rigidity & safety
Bodywork: Carbon-fiber composite panels for strength & lightness
Aerodynamics: Low-drag front wing, sculpted side pods, high-downforce rear wing
Power Unit: Hybrid-electric turbocharged engine with regenerative braking system
Tires: Adaptable compounds (Soft, Medium, Hard) with AI-driven wear prediction
Electronics: Wireless telemetry system for real-time performance monitoring
Innovation: Fully "Unwired" control system (focus on wireless data transfer, futuristic concept)
Livery: Metallic silver with neon-blue accents (to align with Unwired branding)
